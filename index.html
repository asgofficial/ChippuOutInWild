Chippu Out In Wild is a free endless runner arcade game you can play instantly in your browser, no download or install required. Dodge logs, collect power-ups, defeat bosses, and compete for the top spot on the global leaderboard.

Is Chippu Out In Wild free to play?

Yes. Chippu Out In Wild is completely free to play in your browser on desktop or mobile, with no download required.

How do you play Chippu Out In Wild?

Click, tap, or press Space to flap and dodge incoming logs. Grab power-ups along the way and defeat bosses to rack up points on the leaderboard.
